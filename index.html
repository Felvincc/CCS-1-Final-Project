Dumaguete City The city of Gentle People
Dumaguete City is the capital of Negros Oriental. Dumaguete is often referred to as “The City of Gentle People”. This university town’s history is a fascinating mix of intercontinental influences and indigenous traditions. The locals are involved with creative and academic amusements and this is reflected in their daily lives.

Because of the four universities and a number of other colleges established in Dumaguete City, the place is commonly referred to as the University town. The urban center is best known for Silliman University, the country’s first Protestant university and the first American university in Asia.
